To notice not just what people do, but why they do it. Real life is a wellspring of inspiration because it's full of contradictions, enigmas, and emotional truths.

A woman in a bus station can inspire a tale of unrequited love. A second-hand store watch with a broken face can become a time-traveling memento. Writers transform the ordinary into the extraordinary, and that starts by seeing the world differently.
